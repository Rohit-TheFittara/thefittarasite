# PurpleSeam Pre-Launch Readiness Audit

## Purpose
This document defines the **questionnaire**, **scoring logic**, and **recommendation framework** for PurpleSeam’s paid pre-website / go-to-market filtering product.

PurpleSeam is positioned as an **intelligence-first decision platform**, not a website builder.  
The goal is to **prevent founders from making costly channel and infra mistakes**.

---

## Fittara Launch Compass
Fittara's pre-launch assessment to help founders find direction before investing in build or ads.

> "Don't spend on a website. Don't spend on ads.  
> Before you give money to agencies, platforms -- even us, find your direction."

---

## How This Audit Is Used

- Paid diagnostic before any website / platform onboarding
- Founder fills questionnaire (15–20 mins)
- Scores computed automatically
- Human-reviewed verdict (initial phase)
- Clear recommendation + 90-day roadmap

---

## Questionnaire Structure

All questions are scored **0–5**.

---

## Section A: Product Reality (Weight: 25%)

1. Average Order Value (AOV)
2. SKU standardisation
3. Return / exchange risk
4. Visual appeal & storytelling
5. Purchase frequency

---

## Section B: Founder Reality (Weight: 20%)

6. Monthly experimentation budget  
7. Weekly time availability  
8. Ops comfort (shipping, returns)  
9. Comfort with selling / customer interaction  

---

## Section C: Channel Readiness (Weight: 25%)

10. Marketplace experience  
11. Instagram presence  
12. Website readiness  
13. Paid ads experience  

---

## Section D: Unit Economics (Weight: 20%)

14. Gross margin  
15. Shipping + RTO tolerance  
16. Ability to sustain 3 months of experiments  

---

## Scoring Logic

| Dimension | Weight |
|---------|--------|
| Product Reality | 25% |
| Founder Reality | 20% |
| Channel Readiness | 25% |
| Unit Economics | 20% |
| Judgment Buffer | 10% |

### Score Interpretation

- **0–40** → Not ready for website or ads  
- **41–60** → Lean channels only  
- **61–75** → Ready for controlled website  
- **76–100** → Ready to scale  

---

## PurpleSeam Recommendation Mapping

### If NOT Ready for Full Website

**Goal:** Build demand signal, creative-market fit, and lead intelligence.

**Recommended Modules**
- AI Creative Engine
- AI Cataloguing & Quality Layer
- Instagram auto-posting + engagement analysis
- Comment intent detection
- WhatsApp marketing + conversation intelligence

**Not Recommended Yet**
- Full website build
- Paid website ads

---

### If Partially Ready (Lean Website Stage)

**Goal:** Convert warm demand and capture first-party data.

**Recommended Modules**
- Lightweight storefront
- WhatsApp-first checkout
- Retargeting-ready catalog
- SKU prioritisation intelligence

---

### If Fully Ready to Scale

**Goal:** Scale revenue while protecting margins.

**Recommended Modules**
- Full website + checkout
- Conversion API integrations
- Attribution & SKU intelligence
